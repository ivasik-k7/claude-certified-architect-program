# Study Management Commands

## Planning

/study-plan [weeks] [hours-per-week]

- Creates personalized study plan
- Example: /study-plan 8 10
- Action: Generates week-by-week study schedule based on domains

/today-tasks

- Shows what to study today based on plan
- Action: Lists specific lessons/exercises for current day

/weak-areas

- Identifies topics needing more review
- Action: Analyzes quiz results and progress to suggest focus areas

## Review

/review-last-session

- Quick recap of previous study session
- Action: Summarizes last session's key points

/quick-recall [domain]

- Rapid-fire concept review
- Example: /quick-recall 5-context-management
- Action: Presents key terms for quick definition

/mnemonics [concept]

- Creates memory aids for complex topics
- Example: /mnemonics "agentic loop stages"
- Action: Generates memorable acronyms or phrases

## Resource Management

/find-resource [topic]

- Locates relevant resources
- Example: /find-resource "context passing diagrams"
- Action: Searches resources/ directory for matching files

/update-resource [file] [content]

- Updates or adds to resource files
- Example: /update-resource cheatsheet.md "Add MCP protocols section"
- Action: Appends or modifies shared resources

/link-related [file1] [file2]

- Creates cross-references between related content
- Example: /link-related 1-2-multi-agent.md 2-4-mcp-integration.md
- Action: Adds "See Also" sections to both files
